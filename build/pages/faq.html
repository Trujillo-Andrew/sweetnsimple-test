Frequently Asked Questions

Are your cupcakes made daily?

Yes! Our cupcakes are made fresh each morning! We’re there bright and early getting our cupcakes out for the day, and we make additional cupcakes throughout the day as needed.

If I buy more cupcakes will I save more money?

Yes, the more you buy, the more you save! You can mix and match the cupcakes and still take advantage of these savings.

What happens to your left over cupcakes?

If we have left overs at the end of the night, they are donated to local businesses. We never sell day-old cupcakes.

How should I store my cupcakes/cake?

We recommend you store your cupcakes/cakes covered in the refrigerator and if it has buttercream frosting, leave it out at room temperature for 20-30 minutes for the frosting to soften. ENJOY!

What is the shelf life of your cupcakes?

Our cupcakes have a 3 day shelf life. Of course, just like anything they will taste best if they are enjoyed closest to the purchase date.

Do you make Gluten Free, Eggless, or Sugar Free Cupcakes?

No, we do not offer Gluten Free, Eggless or Sugar Free cupcakes at this time.

Are your cupcakes nut-free?

We do use some tree nuts (pistachios, pecans, peanut butter) in some of our products, therefore we can’t guarantee that trace amounts won’t be present in all products. We do however take all precautions to keep our nut products separate from the rest of our inventory.

Can I place an order before I arrive?

Absolutely. Placing an order is the best way to make sure you’re getting the exact cupcakes you want, as our storefront does sell out towards the end of the day. There is no minimum order for cupcake orders. To place an order, you can either call/text us at (970) 639-1177, DM us on our Facebook/Instagram or through this link.

Do you do cupcakes for weddings?

Cupcakes are a popular and practical alternative. We would love to provide you with cupcakes for your special day. We offer taste packs where you can choose 6 of our flavors at a time for you and your fiance to taste test at the comfort of your home. We can customize the cupcakes to coordinate with your wedding. We also offer stand rentals, delivery, and set up.

Do you offer cupcakes for corporate events?

Yes! We would be happy to cater your function. Please contact us for more details and pricing.

Do you sell cakes?

Yes, we make cakes! We offer 4", 6" and 8" 4 layer cakes, YUM! You can order any style cake you would like. You can have a look through our website gallery, Facebook Page, or Instagram for pictures of cakes we have created, or you can message us a photo of a cake you would like us to replicate. We typically need at least 2 days notice for weekday orders and 7 days notice for weekend orders, however we do book up on weekends so the most notice, the better! We also have a selection of ready-to-go cakes you can pickup in the store, on a first-come first-serve basis. WE DO NOT use fondant on any of our cakes.

Do you sell mini cupcakes?

Yes, we do!
