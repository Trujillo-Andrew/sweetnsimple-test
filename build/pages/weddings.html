Weddings

Cupcakes

Cupcakes are a fantastic choice for your wedding reception. You can choose several flavors so each guest has a dessert they love, and these sweet treats make stunning displays on your dessert table. When you order cupcakes from Sweet & Simple Cupcakery, you'll get delicious desserts bursting with flavor and the top-tier service you deserve on your wedding day. You can choose to pick up your cupcakes before your event or enjoy full-service delivery. When we deliver your cupcakes, we'll create beautiful setups wherever you need them so you're free to enjoy your special day.

Pricing

You can choose from our signature cupcakes, mini cupcakes or a mix of both for a visually appealing dessert table. Our signature cupcakes start at $35/dozen, while our mini cupcakes start at $18/dozen. We recommend about 1.5 regular-sized cupcakes per guest or 2.5 mini cupcakes. With frequent updates to our menu, we have a wide range of signature flavors for your event — so if you've tried a cupcake here before, talk to us about serving it at your wedding celebration. To help you find the perfect cupcakes for your day, we offer a bridal four-pack you can sample for pick up so you and your sweetheart can taste test in the comfort of your home.

Cakes

Whether you want a cake for you and your sweetheart or as an additional dessert option for guests, you can order that at Sweet & Simple Cupcakery, too! You can choose from a semi-naked, horizontal ribbed or smooth cake. We offer: Round cakes starting at $40: 4-inch, 6-inch or 8-inch varieties Two-tier round cakes starting at $125: 6-inch base and 4-inch top or 8-inch base and 6-inch top Flowers and toppers are not included in cake sales. You must coordinate this with another vendor. If you supply flowers and toppers, we will apply them to the cake when delivered. Semi – Naked is a rustic frosting technique that fits perfectly with outdoor and barn venues. Horizontal Ribbed is an elegant and classic wedding cake look. Smooth is a sweet & simple but beautiful design for your cake. Your cake will come with a custom Mr. & Mrs *lastname* topper on us for a special thank you for choosing us to be a part of your special day!
