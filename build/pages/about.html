Sweet & Simple

Est. 2022

In 2022, Sweet and Simple Cupcakery came to life through the vision and passion of Connie Aguilar and her wonderful daughters, Miranda and Olivia. It marked the realization of Connie's lifelong dream, allowing her to create a delightful haven for cupcake lovers. At Fruita's beloved cupcakery, every customer gets a taste of heaven, served with love and care.

Together, as proud Fruita residents, they faithfully orchestrate the magic of delicious hometown cupcakes and exceptional customer service, making each visit a truly angelic experience.
